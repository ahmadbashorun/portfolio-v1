ABOUT ME

I'm a UI/UX designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. I have a passion for creating innovative digital products that solve problems and improve the user experience. I believe that great design is not only aesthetically pleasing, but also functional, intuitive, and easy to use.

I am constantly learning new skills, tools, and techniques to enhance my work, and I'm always eager to take on new challenges and projects. Currently, I’m actively working towards being a UX Engineer.

In addition to my work as a designer, I founded a startup called Thrivee to help students access early-career opportunities. I have also worked as a Product Manager at a Product Development studio.

When I'm not building stuff, I enjoy reading and watching tech-related content, exploring new tech gadgets, watching football matches, and playing games.
